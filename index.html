Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working with Prof. Shuai Wang. I received my Ph.D. in 2024 from the Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), where I was advised by Prof. Yanan Cao. Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. degree from Henan University in 2018.

Research interests: Large Language Model, AI Security, Text Generation.
